SEA WATCH RESORT

Hotel Details

Overlooking the Atlantic Ocean, this beachfront resort is 2 miles from shopping at Tanger Outlets and 5 miles from Grand Dunes Golf Club.

The bright, laid-back rooms are housed in 2 towers and come with full kitchens and pull-out sofas, cable TV, free Wi-Fi and balconies. Upgraded rooms have washer/dryers.

Water features include heated pools, hot tubs, lazy rivers and a kids' pool. Dining options consist of a seasonal poolside tiki bar, an ice cream shop and an oceanfront restaurant. There's a business center, an exercise room, meeting space and a game room. A golf department assists with tee times at nearby courses. Parking is free.
